# Account Settings

The Account section in Settings lets you view your profile and manage your contact information.

## Profile Information

These fields are set during account creation and cannot be changed:

- **Username** — your Permission Slip identity, used across the platform.
- **Login email** — the email address you use to sign in, managed through your authentication provider.

## Contact Information

These fields are optional and can be updated at any time:

- **Contact email** — used for email notifications about approval requests. This can be different from your login email.
- **Phone number** — used for SMS notifications. Must be in E.164 format (e.g., `+15551234567`).

Update either field and click **Save Changes** to apply.

<Info>
  If you enable email or SMS notifications without setting the corresponding contact info, you'll see a warning reminding you to add it.
</Info>

## Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA is managed separately from the Account section. To set up or manage MFA, click your avatar in the top-right corner and select **Security**. See [Secure Your Account](/getting-started/secure-your-account) for setup instructions.
